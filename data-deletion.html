How to Request Data Deletion

Users who have connected their Facebook or Instagram account to iig-agent-app may request the deletion of their data at any time. We follow Meta’s User Data Deletion Policy.

1. Automatic Deletion

If you remove our app from your Facebook or Instagram account settings, all data associated with your account will automatically be deleted from our servers within 30 days.

2. Manual Data Deletion Request

If you want us to delete your data manually, please send an email with the subject "Data Deletion Request" to:

Email: ramkumar.8025708@gmail.com

Your request must include:

  • Your name (optional)
  • Your Instagram or Facebook User ID
  • Any additional details needed to identify your account

We will delete all stored data related to your account within 7–30 working days and send a confirmation email once completed.

3. Data We May Store Before Deletion

This may include:

  • Instagram/Facebook User IDs
  • Webhook event logs
  • Automated message/interaction logs
  • Technical logs (error logs, usage logs)

All data is deleted permanently upon request or account disconnection.
